Our Initiatives

A few high-school students from the Willoughby Township have a created a new non-profit organization called Pollinate BC.
The mission of the organization is to engineer custom-made beehives optimized for the harsh BC climate.
In light of the recent, rapid decline in the bee population, and the crucial role they play within the ecosystem, we have launched this cause to ameliorate that issue.
Our plan is to build a few beehives, donate them to different schools, and then let them be apart of their community. Both spreading education, as well as rejuvenating the bee population.
